I am a PhD candidate in Political Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

My research focuses on the political economy of foreign aid, with a particular emphasis on developing countries.

My dissertation examines when and why governments publicize or conceal foreign aid projects. To study this, I construct an original dataset capturing how aid-receiving governments communicate about foreign aid projects through their official channels. This project sheds light on the strategic communication of aid by recipient governments.
